The Experience

Four moments at the bar.

More than a favor table — a live, guided ritual your guests walk through, glass in hand.

Consultation

Guests sample the curated menu through glass cloches and tester vials, guided by our bar assistant to a scent that fits their mood.

Decanting

Their chosen fragrance is decanted right in front of them with gold or silver syringes — part craft, part performance.

Labelling

Each bottle is hand-sealed with a metallic gold foil label bearing your names and event date — a keepsake, not a favor.

Claiming

One stub, one bottle. Your coordinator distributes claim stubs, keeping the flow fair and orderly all night.

Booking Process

Four steps to lock your date.

01

Check availability

Send your event date, venue, preferred bottle size, and scent count so we can confirm the calendar.

02

Reserve with 30%

A 30% non-refundable, transferable downpayment locks in your slot and starts your custom sticker and stub design.

03

Approve the design

We send digital mockups of your gold sticker and claim stub for your review before anything goes to print.

04

Settle the balance

The remaining 70% is due on the event day, before our team sets up the booth.

The Experience

Four moments at the bar.

More than a favor table — a live, guided ritual your guests walk through, glass in hand.

Consultation

Guests sample the curated menu through glass cloches and tester vials, guided by our bar assistant to a scent that fits their mood.

The Transparent "No Leftover" Policy

We believe you should get exactly what you pay for. If any bottles from your package remain unclaimed at the end of our booked service hours, our assistants will fill, cap, seal, and pack the remaining labeled bottles into a pristine storage box and hand them directly to you before we leave the venue.

The Secure Guest Claim Stub System

To guarantee an orderly reception and prevent budget-breaking "double claiming" of favors, our team operates strictly on a "one stub, one bottle" basis. Stubs are provided to your coordinators in advance.

Ingress & Setup Time Requirements

We require access to your venue exactly 1.5 to 2 hours before your reception or program begins, to construct our boutique display, apply your custom gold-foil stickers, and pre-cap every bottle so the bar is ready for rapid, high-speed fills the moment it opens.
